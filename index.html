About Dilli Delights

Dilli Delights was born from a journey of passion, perseverance, and purpose. Founded by Hasan Rajabi, a PhD scholar and dedicated cancer researcher with over 25 years of experience, this restaurant blends the science of precision with the art of cooking. Inspired by his mother’s recipes from Delhi and Banaras, Hasan brings authentic North Indian flavors crafted with dedication and quality. Join us on this delicious journey where every meal feels like home.
